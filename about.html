S N Public School

Vision

To inspire students to become lifelong learners, responsible citizens, and leaders of tomorrow, equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to excel in an ever-changing global landscape.

Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive, and intellectually stimulating learning environment that fosters independent thinking, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning.

About

SN Public School is a premier educational institution that has been providing quality education to students in Jaitpur Ambedkar Nagar since its founding in 2002. The school is run privately and is located in a rural region of Uttar Pradesh's Ambedkar Nagar district. The school's mission is to provide a stimulating atmosphere, opportunities to extend perspectives, and the effortless development of knowledge and skills for students and instructors.

At SN Public School, the focus is not just on imparting textbook information, but on cultivating a sensitive mindset, empathy, and reasonable thought towards the deteriorating health of our Mother Earth so that the stakeholders of the future will have enough to eat. The school is committed to empowering women, particularly the disadvantaged, and to producing globally responsible individuals who can maintain their independence at all times.

The school features excellent facilities, including well-maintained classrooms, separate rooms for non-teaching activities, and a separate room for the school's headmaster and teachers. The school has a cemented perimeter fencing and access to electricity and drinking water, ensuring that the basic needs of the students are met. Additionally, the school has a playground for students to engage in physical activities and sports, promoting a well-rounded education.

At SN Public School, the emphasis is on creating a learning community that is dedicated to providing every child with the finest education possible. The school has developed an atmosphere and environment conducive to learning by providing students with chances to serve as role models and by challenging them to discover their talents and build on them. Overall, SN Public School is a top-notch educational institution that has been shaping the minds of students for over two decades and is committed to providing an exceptional education to its students.

Principal's Desk

Today, a school's job is to teach well and to inspire and equip its students to be lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and valuable members of a global society that is constantly changing. At SN Public School, we create an environment where kids can grow in many ways and are encouraged to use their talents to strive for excellence. It is only possible in a holistic, student-centric environment.

Each student's talents, skills, and abilities need to be identified, nurtured and encouraged so that they can reach greater heights. Students need to be provided with a platform to think, express, and exhibit their skills. It is necessary to empower them to negotiate several issues that confront them, with the teacher being a facilitator.

The school strives to make the best possible efforts to inculcate strong values combined with academics and extracurricular activities in the children. To convert every individual into a self-reliant and independent citizen, the school provides an amalgam of scholastic and co-scholastic activities.

With warm wishes

Raj Bhaskar

About SNPS

The S N PUBLIC SCHOOL, Jaitpur Ambedkar Nagar, was founded in 2002 and is run privately. It is situated in a rural location. It is situated in the RAMNAGAR neighbourhood of the AMBEDKAR NAGAR district in Uttar Pradesh. The school encompasses grades 9 through 12.
