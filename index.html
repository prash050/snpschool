Sports Day

Sports day is a much-awaited event in our school calendar that brings together students, teachers, and staff in a fun-filled day of healthy competition. Our sports day is a celebration of physical abilities, teamwork, and sportsmanship. We encourage our students to embrace the spirit of healthy competition.

Recitation & Extempore

The school recently hosted a Recitation and Extempore event, promoting public speaking and literary skills among students. It encouraged students to share their perspectives on various topics and enhanced their communication skills. Overall, it was an exciting and memorable event.

Science & Technology Day

The school's Science and Technology Day event promoted scientific thinking and innovation among students by showcasing their projects and experiments. The event encouraged creativity and curiosity, making it a huge success. It was an inspiring and educational experience for all participants.

National and cultural festival celebration

The school's National and Cultural Festival celebrated diversity and cultural awareness through colorful dances and music. It was an engaging occasion that fostered a deeper understanding and respect for different traditions, bringing the school community together. The festival was a memorable and meaningful experience that encouraged students to explore and appreciate our country's rich cultural heritage.

About SNPS

The S N PUBLIC SCHOOL, Jaitpur Ambedkar Nagar, was founded in 2002 and is run privately. It is situated in a rural location. It is situated in the RAMNAGAR neighbourhood of the AMBEDKAR NAGAR district in Uttar Pradesh. The school encompasses grades 9 through 12.
