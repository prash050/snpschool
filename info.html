About SNPS

The S N PUBLIC SCHOOL, Jaitpur Ambedkar Nagar, was founded in 2002 and is run privately. It is situated in a rural location. It is situated in the RAMNAGAR neighbourhood of the AMBEDKAR NAGAR district in Uttar Pradesh. The school encompasses grades 9 through 12.
